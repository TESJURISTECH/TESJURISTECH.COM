About Us

Founded in 2012 as a virtual-first enterprise, TESJURISTECH – IT and Legal Consultancy headquarters in Udupi and a workspace near Infopark, Ernakulam, has evolved into a globally recognized knowledge and solutions partner, driven by a multidisciplinary team of 127+ domain experts across digital transformation, emerging technologies, legal advisory, and strategic business consulting.

In IT consultancy, we deliver enterprise modernization, data-driven strategies, cloud-native solutions, cybersecurity, automation, and integration of emerging technologies like AI and blockchain—enabling organizations to scale, innovate, and stay secure.

In legal services, we handle Motor Accident and Railway Claims, Property and Construction disputes, Family Law matters, Service and Labour issues, Civil and Corporate litigation, Legal Documentation, Property Registration, Arbitration, and Mediation—ensuring rights are protected and disputes resolved effectively.
